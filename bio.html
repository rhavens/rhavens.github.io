Hi, my name is Ryan Havens. All my life, I’ve known I wanted to become a programmer.

One day, as I lay in my cradle debugging some C++ classes, it all made perfect sense to me. Programming is my calling, technology my passion, development my labor.

In reality I made my way through my first C++ classes at 14, and today I can write programs in ten different programming languages, with mastery in C++, Java, Ruby, and Python. I have put much effort into developing web applications during my college years - it’s easy to create a helpful, working application in a short period of time, and the rapid pace of development helps keep my creative juices flowing.

At Tufts University, I’ve worked alone, worked with other developers, and even worked with the computer-illiterate. In Spring 2015 I built a student-to-student home rental website, which I continue to improve to this day.

I am proud of creating something that matters to my student body, and potentially students across the country. For my previous internship I developed UI tools for Ab Initio, writing and refactoring more than 5,000 lines of code over the summer.

I bring to the table experience, passion, and self-motivation. I have led and managed my own projects, brought simple concepts to fruition, and I hope I can do the same for you.
